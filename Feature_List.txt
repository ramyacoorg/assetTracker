PROJECT NAME:
AssetTracker – Company Asset Management System

Understanding of Client Requirements:

The client wants a software system to manage company-owned assets and track which asset is currently assigned to which employee. 
The system should help management monitor asset allocation, availability, maintenance status, and reduce asset loss by maintaining proper digital records.

Creative Freedoms Taken:

• Named the product "AssetTracker".
• Assumed this system is an internal company application.
• Designed it as a web-based system for easy access.
• Introduced role-based access for Admin and Employees.
• Added features for asset feedback, requests, and visual dashboards.
• Included notification and alert mechanisms for better asset maintenance.

Assumptions Made:

• Each employee has a unique employee ID.
• Each asset has a unique asset ID.
• Only Admin users can assign or reassign assets.
• One asset can be assigned to only one employee at a time.
• Employees have limited access based on their role.
• Asset requests and communications are private between employees and management.
• The company wants a simple, easy-to-use interface.

Feature List:

Employee Management  
   Allows management to add, update, and remove employee details.

Asset Management  
   Enables management to add, update, and track company assets such as laptops, mobiles, and software licenses.

Asset Assignment and Return  
   Assets can be assigned to employees and marked as returned when no longer in use.

Role-Based User Login  
   Provides secure login for Admin and Employees. Admins manage assets, while Employees have limited access.

Employee Asset View  
   Employees can view the list of assets currently assigned to them.

Asset Feedback and Issue Reporting  
   Employees can submit feedback or report issues related to assigned assets, which is visible to management.

Asset Request and Approval System  
   Employees can request new assets through the system. These requests are visible only to management, who can approve or reject them.

Asset Expiry and Subscription Tracking  
   Tracks asset warranties, service contracts, and software license expiry dates, with alerts shown to management.

Management Dashboard with Visual Analytics  
   Displays charts and visual summaries showing asset status such as assigned, available, and under maintenance.

Notification and Alert System  
    Sends notifications to management for asset issues, expiry alerts, and new asset requests.
